# MindVault 🧠✨

> A sleek, gamified e-learning experience built to make studying less of a chore and more of a win.

## 🎯 What is MindVault?
Let's face it: traditional e-training is often clunky, ineffective, and flat-out boring. MindVault is a desktop application designed to fix that. Built specifically with students in mind—especially freshmen adapting to university—it strips away the confusion of complex interfaces and replaces them with an aesthetically pleasing, interactive environment.

## ✨ Key Features
* **Quick Access Panel:** Jump straight into your courses, quizzes, and progress tracker without digging through nested menus.
* **Gamified Progress:** A "My Progress" dashboard that tracks your high scores and ranks to keep you motivated.
* **Interactive Quizzes:** Real-time feedback with instant color-coded reviews (Green = Great, Red = Needs Work).

## 🛠️ Under the Hood
* **Environment:** C# .NET Framework (Windows Forms)
* **Database:** Lightweight local storage using `users.txt` and `quiz_scores.txt`

## 🎨 Designed for Humans (HCI Principles)
MindVault isn't just about the code; it's heavily focused on user experience. It was built applying core Human-Computer Interaction principles:
* **Midnight Lavender Theme:** A dedicated dark mode to reduce eye strain during late-night study sessions.
* **Minimalist "Flats UI":** No unnecessary borders or visual clutter. Just clean, readable content.
* **System Visibility:** Hover effects and interactive elements ensure you always know exactly what the system is doing.
